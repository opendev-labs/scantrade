Risk & Governance

Understanding the safety layers and health score algorithms.

System Health Score

The System Health Score is a real-time metric (0-100) that governs the operational state of the entire platform. It is calculated using a multi-factor weighted formula:

Drawdown (40%)
Current DD %
Exposure (30%)
Portfolio %
Performance (20%)
Win Rate

Governance Action Levels

  • 80-100 (Optimal): Full execution permitted.
  • 60-79 (Caution): New position sizing reduced by 50%.
  • < 40 (Critical): Trading Auto-Paused. All open signals cancelled.

Hard Risk Limits

Parameter Limit Description
Max Position Size 5% Maximum allocation per single trade.
Max Portfolio Exposure 20% Maximum total capital deployed at any time.
Max Drawdown 10% Daily drawdown circuit breaker.

Automated Circuit Breakers

In the event of high market volatility where correlation between assets exceeds 0.85, the system automatically triggers a Correlation Squeeze Pause, restricting new trades until divergence is detected. This prevents heavy losses during systemic market crashes.
